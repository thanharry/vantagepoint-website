PHONE CONSULTATIONS

Phone Consultations offer a direct, private conversation between a client and an appropriate subject matter expert. Your dedicated VantagePoint Project Manager identifies the business Advisors who best fit your project request and sets up your VantagePoint consultation session via our VantagePoint NOW Portal.

INSIGHTS

Our guided research products are designed to leverage expert perspectives to help you understand key issues and trending topics. Led by a seasoned team, many of whom are former buy-side and sell-side analysts, VantagePoint Insights offers moderated teleconferences and live meetings to give you the right content to reduce research times, navigate complex situations and bring focus to what matters. Transcripts from these events are accessible on the INSIGHTS Library Portal where content can be searched and downloaded.

SURVEYS

Imagine what you could do with information from hundreds of experts. When you field a VantagePoint survey, that’s precisely what you’ll get. Surveys can be a powerful tool in the decision-making process, efficiently delivering insights from a large number of experts in a short period of time.

DATA

Are you ready to get the most out of your data? Our offerings are designed to bring value to sophisticated, data-savvy clients as well as information-focused clients looking to incorporate quantitative data into a fundamental perspective. Our data product options include insights, tracker, data feeds, A/B testing and summary & overview data.

LEGAL ADVICE & SOLUTIONS

Would an expert witness be the key to supporting your case? VantagePoint offers legal advice, legal representation services, expert witness searches and support to match the right expertise to meet your litigation needs. We also review records and perform merit analyses to determine the validity of a claim using the pillars of causation, liability, and damages.

ON-DEMAND SOURCING

At VantagePoint, we are proud to provide our clients with Advisors that match their specific expertise criteria. INTERESTED IN BECOMING AN ADVISOR?

VantagePoint recruits, registers, and rigorously vets each Advisor on their knowledge and expertise prior to accepting them in our network. Once an Advisor is accepted into the network, the VantagePoint team will reach out to the Advisor regarding relevant projects that match their experience based on our clients’ needs.

We value our Advisors. VantagePoint has a dedicated Advisor Relations team to support our Advisors and help them make the most of their experience working with VantagePoint.
